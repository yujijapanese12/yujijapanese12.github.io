Message

I struggled when I was learning English, because Japanese is linguistically so distant from almost all other languages. That challenging experience led me to understand the frustration students face. This is why I became a Japanese teacher.

Lessons

What You're Struggling With

You lack the confidence to speak with native speakers.

You study grammar and vocabulary, but can't use it in conversation.

You want to master practical, natural Japanese.

What You Will Gain

Speak Confidently

You will communicate naturally and confidently with native speakers.

Think in Japanese

You will process ideas directly in Japanese instead of translating from English.

Enjoy Real Conversations

You will use practical, natural Japanese in real-life situations, not just textbook sentences.

Lesson Types

01

Grammar with Phrases (Beginner)

Learn grammar and practice using it right away to build your speaking foundation.

02

Topic Talk (Intermediate)

Choose a topic, prepare the essential vocabulary and phrases, and practice using them in conversation.

03

Free Talk (Advanced)

Chat easily and naturally about anything in a relaxed setting to reach true fluency.
